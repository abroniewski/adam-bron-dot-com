## **Be Young Be Strong**

“Be Young, Be Strong” is a unique series of videos and podcasts entirely produced by ULB Culture with the collaboration of journalist Kerenn Elkaïm. In these capsules, eight authors confide in students about their youthful years in order to send them a message of hope concerning the pandemic which has come to turn upside down this crucial period of their existence. **[Following](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/be-young-be-strong-1)**

## **Tentes culturelles**

Étant donné que nous n'avons toujours pas les nouvelles directives pour la reprise des festivités folkloriques, merci de noter que nous ne prenons que des options et non des réservations. Nous reviendrons vers vous la semaine prochaine dès que nous connaitrons les nouvelles mesures.
Merci aussi de noter que nous donnerons suite à vos requêtes dans les jours à venir étant donné le très haut taux de demandes à traiter. Merci de votre compréhension. **[Suite](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/tentes-culturelles)**

## **[Radio Campus](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/radio-campus)**

Radio Campus is the ULB radio station, broadcasts in 92.1 FM and also via the Internet. The station brings together more than 120 animators and technicians, offers around fifty different programs and stands out as the first independent and associative cultural radio station in Brussels.

**[Find out more](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/radio-campus)**

## **[ULB Culture card](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/carte-ulb-culture)**

The ULB Culture card: your pass to see and hear everything in Brussels and Charleroi! Take advantage of reductions and free places for cultural activities at ULB or with partners.

**[Find out more](https://www.ulb.be/fr/culture/carte-ulb-culture)**

## **[ULB museums](https://musees.ulb.be/fr/le-reseau-des-musees/test-reseau)**

ULB includes several museums with various themes. Open to the general public, they are spread over 4 sites in Brussels, and 2 in Wallonia. Discover them!

**[Find out more](https://musees.ulb.be/fr/le-reseau-des-musees/test-reseau)**

## **[Modern and contemporary art collection](https://www.ulb.be/fr/espaces-culturels/ulb-culture-collection-d-art-moderne-et-contemporain)**

The Modern and Contemporary Art Collection of the Université libre de Bruxelles has nearly 300 works and brings together all forms of modern and contemporary expression, with an emphasis on the diversity of styles and techniques.
