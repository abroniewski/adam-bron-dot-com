## **Benefits**

**Patient**

- Keep track of drugs
- Efficient refills, timing + location reminders
- Drug background, information, instructions
- Shareable history at any pharmacy -> output depends on medtechly pharmacy or other
- On-call pharmacist for support
- Checkins for condition changes

**Pharmacist**

- Completes prescription registration in netMed automatically
- Connect with patient and provide support
- Monitor symptom changes
- Non med-tech, up to date info for drug interactions
- Premium pharmacies get geo reminders

# **10 Aug 2022**

## Proof of Concept - Feature List Questions

1. Do patients want help keeping track of drugs? Are they currently keeping track of them?
2. Do patients forget to get their refills on time? Are they having to visit the pharmacy more than once in a month for refills?
3. Are patients spending time planning their drug pickup logistics (e.g. which pharmacy they are picking up from)?
4. Do patients lookup drug information? Is there benefit that can be provided to patient by having drug info easy to access?
5. Are pharmacist able to provide value to patients if they have access to drug history? Is this value that is recognised by the patient?
6. Can condition changes during drug usage be leveraged by a healthcare team? Change or adapt a course of action? Can it be used by other research organisations?
7. Do pharmacies spend a lot of time updating netMed with drug prescriptions? Is this automated? What impact exists when the prescriptions are out of date? Is there benefit to urgent care centres when unknown patients visit?
