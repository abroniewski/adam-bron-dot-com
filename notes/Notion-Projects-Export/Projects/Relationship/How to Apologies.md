Sarah Style

1. Justify actions to communicate sincerity (i.e. WHY did you do it that way?)
2. Reaffirm love
3. Restitution based on love language (quality time or act of service). Ask how to make restitution

Adam Style

1. Admitting wrong and expressing that you feel poorly about it
2. Verbalize **desire** to modify behaviour to avoid future situation
3. Consider: working together on plan (i.e. ask for Adam's input)
