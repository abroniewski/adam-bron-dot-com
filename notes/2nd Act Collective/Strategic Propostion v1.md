### **4. Value Proposition Design**

Use the _Value Proposition Canvas_ (Strategyzer framework).

#### 4.1. For Participants (Actors, Directors, Volunteers)

- **Jobs to be done:** find creative outlet, learn acting, meet like-minded people, feel belonging.
    
- **Pains:** lack of safe and inclusive spaces; one-off shows; amateur stigma; scheduling fatigue.
    
- **Gains:** confidence, growth, friendships, joy, visibility, healing.
    

#### 4.2. For Audiences

- **Jobs:** seek meaningful entertainment, support local arts, connect socially.
    
- **Pains:** inconsistent quality, unclear branding, inaccessible language.
    
- **Gains:** inspiring, emotionally authentic performances in English; cultural exchange.
    

#### 4.3. For Partners (ITCE, venues, sponsors)

- **Jobs:** strengthen community engagement, cultural presence, and inclusivity.
    
- **Pains:** lack of structured, ongoing programming; capacity limits.
    
- **Gains:** strategic alignment with cultural and community objectives.
