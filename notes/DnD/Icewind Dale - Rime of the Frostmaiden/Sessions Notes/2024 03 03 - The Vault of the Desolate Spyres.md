## **Introduction and Mission Briefing**

- **Guide [[Ilyana]]** assembles the group:
    
    - **[[Sam]] (Bladesinger Wizard)**
    - **[[Kairn Barret]] (Half-Orc Eldritch Knight)**
    - **[[Kai|Kaiden Blackthorn]] (Tiefling Sorcerer)**
    - **[[Myra]] (Eladrin Bard)**
- **Objective**: Travel to the **[[Desolate Spyres]]**, uncover the history of the cataclysm, retrieve the **ancient crystal core**, and return it to the [[Arcanum of the Last Horizon]].
    

---

## **Ambush and Setback**

- The camp is attacked by **raiders and crystalline creatures**, leaving the group with:
    - Scraps of food and water.
    - The **map to the Spyres**, which Ilyana managed to save.
    - All of Ilyana’s research notes were stolen, possibly by the raiders.
- The group chooses to **travel directly to the Spyres**, aiming to beat the raiders to the crystal.

---

## **Journey to the Spyres**

- **Kairn** leads the group to a desert spring, demonstrating survival skills.
    - The group refills supplies, though [[Sam]] humorously uses his ink bottle in the absence of flasks.
    - Tension between [[Sam]] and [[Kai|Kaiden]] flares, with [[Kai|Kaiden]] throwing a bottle at [[Sam]] and bruising his jaw.
- **Crystalline Birds**:
    - The group spots **galewings**, large shimmering birds.
    - [[Sam]] identifies them using his bestiary and warns the party not to look directly at them, as their beauty can be hypnotizing.
- The **Desolate Spyres** come into view.
    - **[[Myra]] teleports [[Sam]]** to the entrance, which is covered in **ancient runes** requiring the balancing of the elements to open.

---

## **Opening the Vault**

- The group balances the elements to unlock the door:
    
    - **Fire**: [[Sam]] uses his fire magic.
    - **Water**: Kairn uses his frost magic.
    - **Earth**: [[Kai|Kaiden]] uses thaumaturgy to create ground tremors.
    - **Air**: [[Myra]] plays her bardic flute.
- The door opens, but [[Myra]] holds Ilyana back and **intimidates her into revealing her motives**:
    
    - Ilyana’s ancestors were responsible for the **cataclysm**, and she seeks the crystal to rectify their mistakes.
    - [[Myra]] attempts to warn the group, but opinions are divided.

---

## **Vault Interior and Combat**

- The group hears a **cry for help** and rushes to a large chamber with pillars, a rectangular pool of water, and **rock-like lizard creatures** and **crystalline scorpions** attacking a lone figure.
    
- **Combat Highlights**:
    
    - Kairn wields his hand axe and Eldritch Knight magic with precision, cutting through the creatures.
    - [[Myra]] uses bardic magic to shatter multiple crystalline enemies.
    - [[Kai|Kaiden]] hurls **exploding ice knives**, showcasing his sorcerous might.
    - [[Sam]] demonstrates his **Bladesinger agility**, vaulting over an enemy and landing a killing blow with his scimitar.
- The **lone figure is revealed to be [[Johnny]]**, a construct created by the vault to test the party's worthiness.
    
    - With a snap of his fingers, Johnny removes the remaining creatures and declares the test complete.

---

## **Visions of Worthiness**

- Johnny probes the minds of the party, revealing their true motivations:
    - **[[Kairn]]**: Escaped an oppressive upbringing in his orc tribe to forge his own destiny.
        - **Verdict**: **WORTHY**.
    - **[[Kai|Kaiden]]**: Haunted by guilt over his reckless use of magic that endangered and killed his younger brother. Protects [[Sam]] as a surrogate for his lost sibling.
        - **Verdict**: **WORTHY**.
    - **[[Sam]]**: Driven by an intense need to prove his worth after struggling to meet expectations at Strixhaven.
        - **Verdict**: **WORTHY**.
    - **[[Myra]]**: Desperately seeks to restore magic to save her dying sister and her people in the Feywild.
        - **Verdict**: **WORTHY**.
    - **[[Ilyana]]**: Revealed to be manipulating the group to acquire the crystal for her own purposes, tied to her family’s culpability in the cataclysm.
        - **Verdict**: **NOT WORTHY**.

---

## **The Crystal’s Judgment**

- A **large sentient crystal** emerges from the pool, containing the essence of a **high arcanist**.
    - It confirms the group’s worthiness and infuses [[Sam]], Kairn, [[Kai|Kaiden]], and [[Myra]] with fragments of its ancient power.
    - Ilyana is **excluded**, her betrayal laid bare.
- The crystal warns that its power is a **trust and responsibility**, tied to the greater mission of restoring balance to the world.

---

## **Aftermath and Reflection**

- The group is visibly shaken by their visions but resolves to continue their journey.
- Johnny leaves them with a cryptic message: “The path forward will not just test your power but your unity.”
- The group’s bond strengthens despite lingering doubts and conflicts.

---

## **Hooks for Future Sessions**

1. **Ilyana’s Fate**: What will the group do with Ilyana? Will her betrayal come back to haunt them?
2. **Crystal’s Power**: Explore the new abilities granted by the crystal and the responsibility of wielding it.
3. **The Cataclysm’s Secrets**: Investigate the truth behind the cataclysm and its connection to the **Arcanum of the Last Horizon**.
4. **[[Kai|Kaiden]] and [[Sam]]’s [[Relationship]]**: Develop the tension between [[Kai|Kaiden]]’s protective instincts and [[Sam]]’s need for independence.
5. **[[Myra]]’s Mission**: Pursue her goal of saving her sister and the Feywild, possibly tied to the group’s larger quest.
6. **Kairn’s Purpose**: Will Kairn discover his true destiny beyond survival and money?

---

## **Session Summary**

The party successfully proved their worthiness, uncovered deeper truths about themselves and Ilyana, and gained fragments of the crystal’s power. The Vault of the Desolate Spyres tested their abilities and motivations, setting the stage for future conflicts and revelations as they continue their quest to mend the shattered world.
