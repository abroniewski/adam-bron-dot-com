
### **Session Summary**

The party confronted the full psychological and magical influence of **Chardalyn** within the Termalaine mines.

- **Kairn Barret** succumbed to the whispers of Chardalyn (“kill, kill, kill”) but resisted at a critical moment, redirecting a killing blow into stone instead of **Myra**.
- The corruption spread:
    - **Kai** turned on **Sam**
    - **Sam** unleashed fire magic against **Emerald**
- The party entered a **multi-layered internal conflict**, with each member struggling against Chardalyn’s pull.

Control was regained through coordinated intervention:

- **Myra** used _Command_ to halt Kai
- **Emerald** restrained Kairn with vines
- **Kairn** regained agency and struck Sam with _Chromatic Orb_ (electricity), disrupting escalation

### **The Ritual Turning Point**

**Emerald and Myra** combined magic in a spontaneous ritual:

- _Moonbeam_ + _Sleep_
- Amplified by:
    - Chardalyn resonance
    - Moonlight from above
    - The Weave itself flowing into the chamber

This created a **purification / suppression effect**:

- All affected characters were put to sleep
- Chardalyn influence was temporarily neutralised
- The environment stabilised

### **Kai’s Transformation**

**Kai made a defining choice:**

- Rejected Chardalyn’s power
- Consciously **gave up magical power**
- Transferred that power to **Myra**

Motivation:

- Fear of impulsivity
- Trauma from brother’s death
- Desire for control, discipline, and clarity

Result:

- Shift from magic → martial identity (archer / disciplined fighter path)
- Major character pivot

### **Dream Sequence (Long Rest)**

Each character experienced symbolic dreams:

- **Emerald**
    - Returned to her grove
    - Felt peace, belonging
    - Saw the party present in that space  
        → Integration of “home” with “found family”
- **Sam**
    - Fire, parents’ death
    - Parents leave → replaced by party  
        → Emotional transference  
        → Let slip: **he may not actually be Sam**
- **Myra**
    - Reflected on newfound power
    - Saw potential to:
        - Help her sister
        - Protect the group  
            → Accepted responsibility

### **End State**

- Party survived Chardalyn corruption event
- All took a **long rest under protective magical field**
- Immediate crisis resolved, but **nothing is truly fixed**
- Decision made:  
    → Travel to **Revel’s End** next
