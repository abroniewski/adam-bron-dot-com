# Turkey Options
## Capadocia
Fly Eindhoven to Goreme (this is the city to visit Cappadocia from, the place with alien looking landscape and balloons). This flight would be 1pm - 1am with 5hr30min layover

## Day Trips
It might make most sense to spend 2-3 days exploring Istanbul city and doing say trips to different places around...
Here is an article where the options below come from [link](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/articles/best-day-trips-from-istanbul)

### Termal
Host springs, waterfalls, lakes, hiking
1.5hrs

### Anadolu Kavagi
2hr ferry ride down Bosphorus river, 3hrs in the city, then 2hr ride back
Ends in Coastal city with small castle

### Princes' Islands
Day trip from Istanbul (leave from kadikoy, inner city, via ferry). Sea side villages and mansion houses

### Belgrad Forest
1hr away
Forests, walks, hikes, picnic

### Kilyos
1.5hrs
Coastal village with beaches. Looks like good summer place.

### Sile and Agva
3.25hrs. Black sea coast, rugged coastline, fishing harbour, meandering rivers
