# Geospatial & Mobility Analytics: How Businesses Can Unlock Hidden Insights

We were working with a digital billboard company aiming to understand traffic patterns near their displays. Our task was to analyze GPS data to determine how long individuals were exposed to each billboard, considering distance and viewing angle. However, the GPS data arrived at varying intervals, presenting a challenge: reconstructing accurate trajectories from these irregular pings.

**The Challenge of Irregular GPS Data**

Without consistent data points, mapping precise movement paths became complex. We needed to estimate not only the duration of exposure but also the exact positioning relative to each billboard. This required advanced analytics capable of handling spatio-temporal data intricacies.

**Leveraging Advanced Analytics with MobilityDB**

To tackle this, we utilized MobilityDB, an open-source geospatial trajectory data management platform built on PostgreSQL and PostGIS. MobilityDB excels at storing and querying complex trajectory data, enabling us to interpolate missing positions and construct continuous movement paths from discrete GPS points. This approach allowed us to accurately assess exposure times and angles, providing the client with valuable insights into audience engagement.

**Why Mobility Analytics Is Essential for Businesses**

In today's dynamic environment, understanding the movement of assets and customers is crucial. Mobility analytics offers insights into:

- **Customer Behavior:** Analyzing movement patterns helps retailers choose optimal locations and tailor marketing strategies.
- **Supply Chain Efficiency:** Monitoring goods in transit enables companies to identify bottlenecks and streamline operations.
- **Urban Planning:** Cities use mobility data to enhance public transportation and reduce congestion.

**Real-World Applications**

Consider how various sectors benefit from geospatial analytics:

- **Logistics Firms:** By integrating geospatial data, companies can optimize delivery routes, reducing fuel consumption and improving service efficiency.
- **Retail Chains:** Analyzing customer movement informs store layout decisions and targeted promotions.
- **Ride-Sharing Services:** Platforms use real-time geospatial data to match drivers with riders efficiently, minimizing wait times.

**Key Data Sources Powering Geospatial Insights**

Effective geospatial analytics relies on diverse data sources:

- **Satellite Imagery:** Offers comprehensive views for environmental monitoring and urban development.
- **GPS Data:** Provides precise location tracking essential for navigation and delivery services.
- **Mobile Data:** Captures individual movement patterns, aiding in retail analysis and public safety initiatives.
- **Social Media Check-Ins:** Reveals popular venues and consumer preferences.

**How AI Is Transforming Geospatial Analytics**

Artificial Intelligence enhances geospatial analytics by:

- **Automating Data Processing:** AI rapidly analyzes large datasets, identifying patterns and anomalies.
- **Enhancing Predictive Analytics:** Machine learning models forecast trends, such as traffic flow and consumer behavior, enabling proactive strategies.
- **Improving Accuracy:** AI refines data precision, reducing errors in mapping and spatial analysis.
