# Background

Shexon is a platform designed to help students secure jobs while aligning with the needs of universities, employers, and government agencies. It extends beyond traditional career support platforms by providing context-aware job matching, visa sponsorship filtering, resume optimization, mentorship programs, and data-driven insights that benefit all stakeholders.

The software will serve as the digital backbone for Shexon's broader initiatives, including career events, mentorship programs, workshops, and job placement support. This roadmap outlines the platform’s core functionalities across multiple stakeholder groups and defines the user stories that will guide development.

# Objectives

- **For Students:** Get a competitive edge in the job market with AI-powered career guidance, tailored resume feedback, and exclusive job opportunities that align with individual skills and visa requirements. Shexon streamlines job searching, interview preparation, and professional networking, making it easier to secure meaningful employment.
- **For Universities:** Enhance student career success and boost institutional rankings by tracking student engagement, analyzing employment outcomes, and strengthening industry partnerships. Shexon helps universities demonstrate ROI on career services, increasing student retention and funding opportunities.
- **For Employers:** Gain direct access to a pipeline of highly qualified, job-ready candidates. Shexon enables targeted job postings, automated candidate filtering based on skills and sponsorship needs, and engagement through mentorship programs, reducing hiring time and costs.
- **For Government Agencies:** Make data-driven policy decisions with real-time insights into student retention, workforce trends, and industry talent shortages. Shexon supports workforce planning and economic growth by aligning academic output with labor market needs.

# Detailed Scope and Feature Overview

## Career Development & Student Support

**Primary User: Students**  
**Goal:** Help students find jobs and improve career readiness.

### AI-Powered Career Guidance Chatbot

1. **Student Q&A on Career Topics:** The chatbot uses a **predefined knowledge base** sourced from university career centers and industry guidelines. Responses include **direct references** to source materials to build credibility and transparency.
2. **Escalating to Career Services:** The chatbot integrates with an **automated meeting scheduler** that syncs with career advisors' calendars, ensuring students can book **available slots in real-time**. The system also **summarizes the student's chat history** to provide career advisors with context before the meeting.
3. **Tracking Career Questions & Trends:** All interactions with the chatbot are **stored and analyzed** using **machine learning models** to identify trends in student inquiries. This helps universities **update their knowledge base** and reduce repeat questions by providing better self-serve resources.

### Resume & Application Review

4. **Uploading a Resume for Review:** Students can upload PDFs, and AI will parse the document using **natural language processing (NLP)**.
5. **Receiving Resume Feedback:** The system applies **rule-based and ML-based scoring** to highlight areas of improvement, such as missing keywords, unclear formatting, or weak experience descriptions.
6. **Identifying Skills Missing from Student Resumes:** AI compares uploaded resumes against job descriptions and identifies skill gaps. The system can **highlight specific certifications or training** that students are missing if they are explicit requirements in job postings. This allows universities to **integrate relevant certifications and training into their curriculum**.

### Job Interview Preparation

7. **Mock Interview Question Generator:** AI dynamically generates **custom interview questions** based on the job role and industry.
8. **Interview Coaching & Best Practices:** The chatbot provides **real-time feedback** on student responses based on predefined best practices.
9. **Employer Feedback on Student Interview Performance:** Employers provide **structured feedback** on student interviews, helping career services refine student preparation programs.

### Mentorship & Networking Matching

10. **Finding a Mentor:** Students are matched with industry mentors based on career interests.
11. **Discovering Networking Events:** Students can find career events relevant to their field.
12. **Industry Mentorship Programs Integrated with Universities:** Universities coordinate mentorship opportunities between students and employers.

### Visa & Sponsorship Support for International Students

13. **Understanding Visa & Work Sponsorship Options:** Job postings indicate whether they offer visa sponsorship.
14. **Step-by-Step Visa Application Guide:** A structured, **interactive guide** walks students through the visa sponsorship process with **country-specific information**.
