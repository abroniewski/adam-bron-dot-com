## Slide 2: The Problem – Barriers to Student Employment

### Students

- Struggle to find jobs that align with their skills and visa requirements
- Lack access to **personalized career guidance** and **resume feedback**
- Need better preparation for **job interviews and networking**

### Universities

- Need better ways to **track student career outcomes**
- Lack insight into the **effectiveness of career services**
- Struggle to provide **real-time career resources** aligned with student needs

### Employers

- Face difficulty **finding the right student candidates**
- Need better ways to **engage with universities** for hiring
- Want to **streamline recruitment and reduce hiring time**

### Government Agencies

- Need insights into **student retention and workforce trends**
- Lack **data-driven reports** on employment gaps and skill shortages
- Require better **alignment between academic programs and labor market needs**

## Slide 3: The Shexon Solution

- **For Students:** AI-powered career guidance, resume review, and job matching
- **For Universities:** Career tracking, student engagement insights, and workshop optimization
- **For Employers:** Direct access to job-ready students, skill-based candidate matching, and recruitment analytics
- **For Government:** Data-driven reports on employment trends, talent retention, and workforce gaps

## Slide 4: Key Features Overview

### AI-Powered Career Guidance Chatbot

- Answers **student career-related questions** using a university-backed knowledge base
- Suggests **job opportunities and resume improvements**
- Automates **meeting scheduling with career advisors**, summarizing student concerns
- Tracks **career-related questions to identify trends** for universities

---

## Slide 5: Resume & Job Matching System

- Students **upload resumes** for AI-driven feedback
- AI compares **student resumes to job descriptions** and suggests **specific improvements**
- Identifies **missing certifications and skills**, allowing universities to integrate them into their programs

---

## Slide 6: AI-Driven Interview Preparation

- AI generates **custom mock interview questions** based on target roles
- Students **practice real-time interviews** through **AI-powered voice chat**
- AI provides **coaching and feedback** at the end of each response

---

## Slide 7: Career Services Dashboard

- Tracks **student engagement with career services**
- Identifies **key support areas** where additional resources are needed
- Recommends **high-demand workshop topics** based on student interactions

## Slide 8: Job Board & Employer Matching

- Students filter jobs based on **visa sponsorship, industry, and location**
- AI matches students to **employers based on past successful hires**
- Employers **search for students by skillset and experience**

## Slide 9: Government & Policy Reporting

- Tracks **student employment outcomes** and **regional retention trends**
- Identifies **skill shortages and gaps in job applications vs. postings**
- Provides policymakers with **data-driven insights into workforce development**
