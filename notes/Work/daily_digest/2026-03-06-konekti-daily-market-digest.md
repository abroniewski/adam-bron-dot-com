# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 2026-03-06

## 1) What stood out
Two themes converged today: process-intelligence vendors are reframing AI around **operational control** (SAP Signavio with Joule GA and UiPath AgentOps) while buyers are tightening **risk/procurement gates** (Reuters contracting signal and CPO governance data) ([SAP Signavio Joule GA](https://news.sap.com/japan/2026/03/0303_process-conversation-joule-sap-signavio-solutions-generally-available/), [UiPath AgentOps](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/agent-ops-operationalizing-ai-agents-for-enterprise), [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/ai-contract-restrictions-could-threaten-military-missions-us-official-says-2026-03-03/), [ProcureCon study](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/industry-study-cpos-are-taking-charge-of-ai-risk-and-growth-in-2026-302703015.html)).

## 2) Coherent storyline
The market is moving from “AI feature launches” to “AI that can be governed in production”: SAP is linking transformation outcomes to orchestration and process conversation layers ([SAP orchestration model](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/orchestrating-continuous-transformation-stronger-business-outcomes/)), UiPath is formalizing AgentOps for runtime control ([UiPath AgentOps](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/agent-ops-operationalizing-ai-agents-for-enterprise)), and data-stack players are exposing programmable transformation/activation primitives that agents can call ([Microsoft Fabric Power Query API preview](https://blog.fabric.microsoft.com/en-US/blog/execute-power-query-programmatically-in-microsoft-fabric/), [Fivetran reverse ETL](https://www.fivetran.com/blog/what-is-reverse-etl)).

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder (single)
**Chief Procurement Officer (CPO):** procurement is now an AI adoption gatekeeper because compliance posture and supplier controls are becoming first-class decision criteria ([ProcureCon 2026 CPO study](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/industry-study-cpos-are-taking-charge-of-ai-risk-and-growth-in-2026-302703015.html), [Reuters contracting constraints](https://www.reuters.com/business/ai-contract-restrictions-could-threaten-military-missions-us-official-says-2026-03-03/)).

- **Apromore:** no high-confidence fresh primary update surfaced in this scan window; monitor for direct product/release statements before inferring strategy shifts.
