# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 2026-03-05

## 1) What stood out
The strongest immediate signal is that enterprise process players are moving from AI add-ons to operating-system behavior: SAP put [Joule into general availability for SAP Signavio workflows](https://news.sap.com/japan/2026/03/0303_process-conversation-joule-sap-signavio-solutions-generally-available/), while UiPath doubled down on [AgentOps as a production discipline](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/agent-ops-operationalizing-ai-agents-for-enterprise).

## 2) Coherent storyline
Across process, data, and governance lanes, the direction is consistent: buyers now expect execution, controls, and measurable rollout in one package. SAP is narrating [continuous transformation through orchestration](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/orchestrating-continuous-transformation-stronger-business-outcomes/), UiPath is pushing [agentic adoption actions for 2026](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/cn/adopting-agentic-ai-2026-things-you-can-do-right-now), and data-stack plumbing is becoming more agent-ready via [programmatic Power Query execution in Microsoft Fabric](https://blog.fabric.microsoft.com/en-US/blog/execute-power-query-programmatically-in-microsoft-fabric/).
