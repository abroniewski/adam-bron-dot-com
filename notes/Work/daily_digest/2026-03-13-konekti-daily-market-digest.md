# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 2026-03-13

## 1) What stood out
This cycle’s strongest pattern is **enterprise AI with procurement-grade controls**: SAP moved next-gen Ariba into availability while UiPath emphasized agent security posture and Celonis pushed composable AI value narratives ([SAP Ariba](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/next-gen-sap-ariba-foundation-for-intelligent-procurement/), [UiPath AIUC-1](https://www.uipath.com/blog/product-and-updates/aiuc-1-certification-next-chapter-trusted-agentic-automation), [Celonis press](https://www.celonis.com/news/press/global-enterprises-unlock-billions-building-composable-ai-solutions-with-celonis)).

## 2) Coherent storyline
Market momentum is shifting from generic “AI features” to **operationally governed deployment**: procurement and compliance are now central to adoption timing ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/ai-contract-restrictions-could-threaten-military-missions-us-official-says-2026-03-03/), [ProcureCon study](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/industry-study-cpos-are-taking-charge-of-ai-risk-and-growth-in-2026-302703015.html)), while platform vendors package execution architecture as the answer (SAP source-to-pay rebuild, UiPath advanced-agent runtime framing, Celonis composable AI positioning) ([SAP Ariba](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/next-gen-sap-ariba-foundation-for-intelligent-procurement/), [UiPath Technical Tuesday](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/technical-tuesday-standard-agents-vs-advanced-agents-whats-different-and-why), [Celonis press](https://www.celonis.com/news/press/global-enterprises-unlock-billions-building-composable-ai-solutions-with-celonis)).

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder (single)
**Chief Procurement Officer (CPO):** buying authority is increasingly tied to AI risk controls, contract terms, and supplier trust evidence, not just feature breadth ([ProcureCon study](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/industry-study-cpos-are-taking-charge-of-ai-risk-and-growth-in-2026-302703015.html), [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/ai-contract-restrictions-could-threaten-military-missions-us-official-says-2026-03-03/)).

## 4) Competitive watch (Celonis/Signavio/UiPath/Apromore)
- **Celonis:** doubled down on AI value realization and supply-chain outcomes with fresh messaging on composable AI and metrics-based visibility ([Celonis press](https://www.celonis.com/news/press/global-enterprises-unlock-billions-building-composable-ai-solutions-with-celonis), [Celonis supply-chain playbook](https://www.celonis.com/blog/supply-chain-metrics-monitoring)).
- **SAP Signavio:** Joule GA signal indicates stronger in-flow AI assistance inside process intelligence workflows ([SAP Signavio Joule GA](https://news.sap.com/japan/2026/03/0303_process-conversation-joule-sap-signavio-solutions-generally-available/)).
- **UiPath:** emphasis on advanced-agent runtime plus AIUC-1 trust credentials strengthens “safe enterprise agentic automation” positioning ([UiPath Technical Tuesday](https://www.uipath.com/blog/ai/technical-tuesday-standard-agents-vs-advanced-agents-whats-different-and-why), [UiPath AIUC-1](https://www.uipath.com/blog/product-and-updates/aiuc-1-certification-next-chapter-trusted-agentic-automation)).
- **Apromore:** no clearly fresh March primary release signal surfaced in this scan window; continue monitoring while avoiding inference without new official update.

## 5) Agentic data-model tooling watch (Illumex + peers)
Two practical signals: programmable transformation in Fabric strengthens machine-acted data prep workflows ([Microsoft Fabric](https://blog.fabric.microsoft.com/en-US/blog/execute-power-query-programmatically-in-microsoft-fabric/)); and Illumex appears in a syndicated acquisition claim that suggests consolidation pressure, but this should remain “watch-only” until primary confirmation from NVIDIA/Illumex ([Illumex syndicated claim](https://portal.sina.com.hk/finance/finance-mediaoutreachnewswire/media-outreach/2026/03/04/1577740/cardumen-capital-strengthens-global-reach-through-its-taipei-based-apac-partner-following-nvidias-acquisition-of-its-portfolio-company-illumex/)).

## 6) Key implications (max 3 bullets)
- Prioritize **procurement-trust messaging** in outreach (controls, contract clarity, governance evidence) as a first-meeting wedge ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/ai-contract-restrictions-could-threaten-military-missions-us-official-says-2026-03-03/), [ProcureCon study](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/industry-study-cpos-are-taking-charge-of-ai-risk-and-growth-in-2026-302703015.html)).
