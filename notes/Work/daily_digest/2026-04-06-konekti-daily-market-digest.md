# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 6 April 2026

## 1) What stood out (short)
UiPath’s combo of [fiscal 2026 results](https://ir.uipath.com/news/detail/431/uipath-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-fiscal-2026-financial-results) (ARR up 11% to $1.853B plus a new $500M buyback) and today’s [agentic orchestration webinar](https://www.morningstar.com/news/business-wire/20260331437800/uipath-to-showcase-latest-agentic-business-orchestration-capabilities-and-product-strategy-in-upcoming-webinar) signals they’re spending balance-sheet credibility to own the “controlled AI agents” narrative before rivals can reframe it.

## 2) Coherent storyline (brief)
Celonis’ [2026 Process Optimization Report](https://www.celonis.com/insights/reports/process-optimization) and the companion [VentureBeat analysis](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/enterprise-agentic-ai-requires-a-process-layer-most-companies-havent-built) highlight that only 19% of enterprises run multi-agent systems despite 85% aiming to go agentic, so platform vendors are racing to prove they provide the missing process context while regulators (per [OneTrust’s EU Digital Omnibus brief](https://www.onetrust.com/blog/how-the-eu-digital-omnibus-reshapes-ai-act-timelines-and-governance-in-2026/)) lock in immovable compliance dates.

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder (single)
**Enterprise operations leaders accountable for AI-enabled transformation programs** — they must reconcile aggressive agentic roadmaps (Celonis/UiPath) with tangible compliance milestones and documented telemetry before August–December 2027 enforcement kicks in.

## 4) Competitive watch (Celonis / SAP Signavio / UiPath / Apromore)
- **UiPath** is pairing solid ARR growth and profitability with a same-day [product strategy broadcast](https://www.morningstar.com/news/business-wire/20260331437800/uipath-to-showcase-latest-agentic-business-orchestration-capabilities-and-product-strategy-in-upcoming-webinar), giving sellers new talking points while reminding customers they can finance outcome-based deals via that [new buyback-fueled balance sheet](https://ir.uipath.com/news/detail/431/uipath-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-fiscal-2026-financial-results).
- **Celonis** is leaning into the “agentic readiness gap,” using its [Process Optimization Report](https://www.celonis.com/insights/reports/process-optimization) to argue that process intelligence is the prerequisite for AI ROI.
- **SAP Signavio** just opened registration for the [Transformation Excellence Summit 2026](https://www.signavio.com/events/sap-transformation-excellence-summit-2026/), synchronizing Atlanta and Berlin events that tie AI narratives directly to value realization workshops — expect fresh playbooks by Q4 pipeline season.
- **Apromore (now inside Salesforce)** surfaced via [equity-award disclosures](https://www.ad-hoc-news.de/boerse/news/ueberblick/salesforce-inc-stock-faces-continued-pressure-after-sharp-6-percent-drop/68989838), signalling CRM-native packaging despite Salesforce’s valuation pressure — watch for bundled offers targeting process mining laggards.

## 5) Agentic data-model tooling watch (Illumex + peers)
- NVIDIA’s ~$75M pickup of Illumex per [Cardumen Capital’s note](https://www.prnewswire.com/apac/news-releases/cardumen-capital-strengthens-global-reach-through-its-taiwan-based-apac-partner-following-nvidias-acquisition-of-its-portfolio-company-illumex-302708212.html) confirms that semantic data fabrics are no longer niche; GPU vendors now see them as essential agent context layers.
- Microsoft is rolling out a [Database Hub inside Fabric](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/fabcon-and-sqlcon-2026-unifying-databases-and-fabric-on-a-single-data-platform/) with Fabric IQ agent workflows, making governed semantic models turnkey for 31k+ customers.
- Google published a [Gemini agent-skill playbook](https://developers.googleblog.com/closing-the-knowledge-gap-with-agent-skills/) plus an evaluation harness to keep SDK knowledge fresh, telegraphing how lightweight semantic overlays will spread through agent builders.

## 6) Key implications (max 3 bullets)
- **Compliance clocks are fixed:** OneTrust’s [Digital Omnibus update](https://www.onetrust.com/blog/how-the-eu-digital-omnibus-reshapes-ai-act-timelines-and-governance-in-2026/) plus Baker Botts’ [energy-sector brief](https://www.bakerbotts.com/thought-leadership/publications/2026/march/the-eu-ai-act) anchor Nov 2026–Aug 2026 enforcement windows, pushing buyers to demand auditable process telemetry now.
- **Process context is the new moat:** Celonis’ data-backed gap narrative and UiPath’s agentic webinar blitz show that whoever operationalizes process intelligence fastest will dictate procurement criteria.
- **Semantic stacks are consolidating:** Illumex joining NVIDIA and Microsoft/Goolge upgrades indicate that “agent-ready” data layers will be bundled into hyperscaler and silicon GTM motions, squeezing smaller middleware vendors.
