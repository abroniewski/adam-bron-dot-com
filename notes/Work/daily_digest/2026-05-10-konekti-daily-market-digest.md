# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 2026-05-10

## 1) What stood out
The strongest signal today is that process intelligence vendors are moving the conversation from dashboards to copilots and agentic orchestration, with [Celonis](https://www.celonis.com/news/press/celonis-recognized-as-a-leader-in-the-2026-gartner-magic-quadrant-for-process-intelligence), [UiPath](https://www.uipath.com/newsroom/uipath-achieves-aiuc-1-certification), and [Apromore](https://apromore.com/blog/meet-your-new-assistant-apromore-copilot) all framing trust, automation, and copilots as the new buying surface.

## 2) Coherent storyline
The market is converging on a simple story: buyers want AI that can act, but only when it sits on top of verified process context and governance; that is visible across [UiPath’s WorkFusion acquisition](https://www.uipath.com/newsroom/uipath-acquires-workfusion-strengthening-agentic-solutions-for-financial-services), [Apromore’s process-intelligence copilot messaging](https://apromore.com/blog/from-static-models-to-conversational-intelligence-using-apromore-copilot-across-the-process-improvement-lifecycle), and [Alation’s governance-first framing for AI agents](https://www.alation.com/blog/ai-agents-trusted-data-success/).

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder
The most impacted stakeholder is the enterprise buyer in regulated operations — especially process, risk, and data governance leaders — because the decision is shifting from feature comparison to proof of control, compliance, and operational fit.

## 4) Competitive watch
- [Celonis](https://www.celonis.com/news/press/celonis-recognized-as-a-leader-in-the-2026-gartner-magic-quadrant-for-process-intelligence) keeps the category-creation narrative strong by pairing analyst validation with process-intelligence leadership.
- [UiPath](https://www.uipath.com/newsroom/uipath-achieves-aiuc-1-certification) is leaning into certification and trust while [its WorkFusion deal](https://www.uipath.com/newsroom/uipath-acquires-workfusion-strengthening-agentic-solutions-for-financial-services) deepens financial-services coverage.
- [Apromore](https://apromore.com/blog/the-future-has-arrived-unlock-agentic-ai-with-process-intelligence) is pushing hard on agentic AI + process intelligence as the next workflow layer.

## 5) Agentic data-model tooling watch
- [Apromore Copilot across the lifecycle](https://apromore.com/blog/from-static-models-to-conversational-intelligence-using-apromore-copilot-across-the-process-improvement-lifecycle) suggests analysts want conversational access to process insights, not just static models.
- [Apromore Task Mining](https://apromore.com/blog/unlocking-full-spectrum-process-intelligence-with-apromore-task-mining) matters because task data can close the gap between observed and modeled work.
- [Alation’s compliance and security guidance](https://www.alation.com/blog/building-data-compliance-framework-comprehensive-guide/) is a reminder that agentic tooling still needs an enforceable data-governance layer.

## 6) Key implications
- Trust is becoming a product feature, not a sales objection, especially in regulated accounts.
- Copilots are moving from “nice UX” to the front door for process intelligence.
- Governance and compliance tooling should be positioned as enablers of agentic execution, not separate initiatives.
