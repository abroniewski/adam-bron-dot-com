# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 2026-04-17

## 1) What stood out (short)
Celonis’ fresh docs around [OCDM versioning](https://docs.celonis.com/en/version-deploy-ocdm.html) and [package deployment](https://docs.celonis.com/en/version-deploy.html) suggest the platform is tightening operational discipline while SAP keeps pushing its [Business AI](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/sap-business-ai-release-highlights-q1-2026/) and [innovation-award](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/top-innovators-2026-sap-innovation-award-finalists/) narrative toward agentic execution.

## 2) Coherent storyline (brief)
The market is converging on one message: process context plus governed execution is the entry ticket for enterprise AI, and that shows up across [Celonis](https://docs.celonis.com/en/creating-and-managing-machine-learning-applications.html), [SAP](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/honoring-2026-sap-innovation-award-winners/), and [Apromore](https://apromore.com/blog/from-hype-to-operational-reality-key-takeaways-from-icpm-2026), while compliance pressure from [OneTrust](https://www.onetrust.com/blog/how-the-eu-digital-omnibus-reshapes-ai-act-timelines-and-governance-in-2026/) and [Baker Botts](https://www.bakerbotts.com/thought-leadership/publications/2026/march/the-eu-ai-act) keeps turning “nice-to-have” telemetry into a procurement requirement.

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder (single)
**Enterprise operations and transformation leaders** — they now have to justify AI rollouts with both execution control and audit-ready governance, not just speed or feature breadth.

## 4) Competitive watch (Celonis / SAP Signavio / UiPath / Apromore)
- **Celonis** is still sharpening the platform story: [OCDM versioning](https://docs.celonis.com/en/version-deploy-ocdm.html), [package deployment](https://docs.celonis.com/en/version-deploy.html), and [skills/action-flows execution](https://docs.celonis.com/en/execute-action-flows-in-skills.html) all point to a more managed operating model for process apps.
- **SAP Signavio** is being carried by broader SAP messaging: [Business AI release highlights](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/sap-business-ai-release-highlights-q1-2026/) and the [2026 innovation awards](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/honoring-2026-sap-innovation-award-winners/) keep linking AI value to transformation outcomes.
- **Apromore** is leaning into the “operational reality” frame from [ICPM 2026 takeaways](https://apromore.com/blog/from-hype-to-operational-reality-key-takeaways-from-icpm-2026), which is a useful counter-position to pure demo-driven process mining.

## 5) Agentic data-model tooling watch (Illumex + peers)
- The strongest adjacent signal today is not a new Illumex headline but the broader “governed semantics” trend: Celonis’ [machine-learning app lifecycle](https://docs.celonis.com/en/creating-and-managing-machine-learning-applications.html) and [skill execution](https://docs.celonis.com/en/execute-action-flows-in-skills.html) imply more structured data/logic layers for agents.
- SAP’s [Business AI](https://news.sap.com/2026/04/sap-business-ai-release-highlights-q1-2026/) and award messaging reinforce the same direction: packaged semantic context, not raw model access, is becoming the commercial story.
- Net: the market is still moving toward data layers that can justify agent actions, even when the vendor name isn’t Illumex.

## 6) Key implications (max 3 bullets)
- [OneTrust](https://www.onetrust.com/blog/how-the-eu-digital-omnibus-reshapes-ai-act-timelines-and-governance-in-2026/) and [Baker Botts](https://www.bakerbotts.com/thought-leadership/publications/2026/march/the-eu-ai-act) keep pushing buyers toward auditable AI, so process telemetry is now part of the sales conversation.
- Celonis and SAP are both using platform-level language to defend control and execution, which makes “workflow + governance” the likely default buyer expectation.
- Apromore’s [operational reality](https://apromore.com/blog/from-hype-to-operational-reality-key-takeaways-from-icpm-2026) framing is a reminder that the market will reward concrete outcomes over abstract agent hype.
