# Konekti Daily Market Digest — 27 Mar 2026

## 1) What stood out
Celonis, SAP, UiPath, and Salesforce/Apromore all moved in lockstep on procurement intelligence this week, while U.S. federal buyers simultaneously floated an intrusive AI clause that could reshape how every agentic vendor proves control [[Celonis AGA win](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/901683981/celonis-wins-2026-aga-innovation-challenge-at-technology-transformation-summit)] [[SAP Ariba GA](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/next-gen-sap-ariba-foundation-for-intelligent-procurement/)] [[UiPath P2P](https://ir.uipath.com/news/detail/438/uipath-announces-new-agentic-solution-to-accelerate-procurement-cycles)] [[GSA clause](https://federalnewsnetwork.com/acquisition-policy/2026/03/gsas-new-ai-clause-drives-contractors-to-sound-the-alarm/)].

## 2) Coherent storyline
Government buyers validated Celonis’ audit controls as “best-in-show,” SAP shipped the rebuilt Ariba backbone, UiPath layered agentic orchestration on purchase-to-pay, and Salesforce locked in Apromore talent—together signaling that enterprise procurement stacks are being rebuilt around AI-governed execution just as regulators (GSA clause, Zifino/Carahsoft partnership) insist on deterministic compliance proof [[Celonis release notes](https://docs.celonis.com/en/march-2026-release-notes.html)] [[Apromore RSUs](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/CRM/salesforce-grants-equity-awards-to-apromore-under-its-inducement-e1fwcu55759t.html)] [[Zifino-Carahsoft](https://www.carahsoft.com/news/zifino-and-carahsoft-partner-to-bring-ai-native-cmmc-compliance-to-the-public-sector-2026)].

## 3) Most impacted stakeholder
Procurement & risk leaders running regulated supply chains—they now face simultaneous pressure to modernize exec layers with agentic tools while documenting AI provenance down to clause-level evidence.

## 4) Competitive watch (Celonis / Signavio / UiPath / Apromore)
- **Celonis:** Federal CFOs crowned its Audit Acceleration solution at AGA’s summit, and the March release added granular AI credit caps plus structured Copilot output—useful proof that Celonis can promise budget discipline to agencies wary of runaway model usage [[AGA win](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/901683981/celonis-wins-2026-aga-innovation-challenge-at-technology-transformation-summit)] [[Release notes](https://docs.celonis.com/en/march-2026-release-notes.html)].
- **SAP Signavio / Ariba:** bpExperts is already productizing an AI-native knowledge graph to make Signavio + Cloud ALM “think” instead of just sync, while SAP formally turned on next-gen Ariba atop BTP—meaning SAP can now pitch an end-to-end, Joule-infused source-to-pay spine with live architecture intelligence [[bpExperts](https://www.bpexperts.de/newsfeed/2026/3/20/from-pipe-to-brain-rethinking-signaviocalm-integration-with-ai-native-architecture)] [[SAP Ariba GA](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/next-gen-sap-ariba-foundation-for-intelligent-procurement/)].
- **UiPath:** The new agentic Purchase-to-Pay solution adds an execution layer over existing ERPs, promising touchless invoice routing and exception triage—directly colliding with SAP’s promise of Joule-native procurement [[UiPath P2P](https://ir.uipath.com/news/detail/438/uipath-announces-new-agentic-solution-to-accelerate-procurement-cycles)].
- **Apromore (Salesforce):** Salesforce granted four-year RSUs to newly acquired Apromore staff, signaling it wants tight retention while fusing process mining IP into Agentforce; expect accelerated roadmap announcements once earn-outs stabilize [[Apromore RSUs](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/CRM/salesforce-grants-equity-awards-to-apromore-under-its-inducement-e1fwcu55759t.html)].

## 5) Agentic data-model tooling watch (Illumex + peers)
- **Illumex:** Cardumen Capital’s post-exit briefing frames Illumex’s semantic layer as a core building block for NVIDIA’s enterprise agent roadmap, implying NVIDIA will push that context stack deeper into autonomous data-model orchestration [[Cardumen / Illumex](https://www.malaysiaworldnews.com/cardumen-capital-strengthens-global-reach-through-its-taipei-based-apac-partner-following-nvidias-acquisition-of-its-portfolio-company-illumex/)].
- **Reg-side peers:** CUBE’s Microsoft Foundry integration and Marketplace launch gives financial institutions an AI-native regulatory graph they can buy through existing Azure procurement rails, offering a complementary governance layer to Illumex-style semantic stacks [[CUBE + Microsoft](https://www.resultsense.com/news/2026-03-26-cube-microsoft-partner-ai-regulatory-compliance)].

## 6) Key implications
- **Budget-proofing AI operations:** Celonis’ asset-level AI quota caps and Salesforce’s talent lock-ins show buyers will expect consumption guardrails before expanding spend—Konekti should demo similar controls alongside any agentic workflows [[Celonis release notes](https://docs.celonis.com/en/march-2026-release-notes.html)] [[Apromore RSUs](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/CRM/salesforce-grants-equity-awards-to-apromore-under-its-inducement-e1fwcu55759t.html)].
- **Procurement modernization is accelerating:** With SAP Ariba’s rebuild live and UiPath inserting agents over legacy ERPs, the competitive window to position Konekti as the neutral orchestration layer is narrowing; we need packaged plays that plug into both ecosystems [[SAP Ariba GA](https://news.sap.com/2026/03/next-gen-sap-ariba-foundation-for-intelligent-procurement/)] [[UiPath P2P](https://ir.uipath.com/news/detail/438/uipath-announces-new-agentic-solution-to-accelerate-procurement-cycles)].
- **Compliance becomes the gate:** Zifino/Carahsoft’s deterministic evidence platform plus GSA’s sweeping AI clause mean enterprises will favor partners who can surface clause-level lineage; integrating clause-aware reporting into our agentic flows becomes a must-have [[Zifino-Carahsoft](https://www.carahsoft.com/news/zifino-and-carahsoft-partner-to-bring-ai-native-cmmc-compliance-to-the-public-sector-2026)] [[GSA clause](https://federalnewsnetwork.com/acquisition-policy/2026/03/gsas-new-ai-clause-drives-contractors-to-sound-the-alarm/)].
